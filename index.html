Project Definition

This work does not begin from religion as identity, nor doctrine as inherited certainty.

It begins from a more fundamental question: the question of God.

Through textual-historical analysis, this project examines the relationship between revelation, transmission, interpretation, and the human understanding of divine reality.

“My pen is the witness / and you are the judges.”
